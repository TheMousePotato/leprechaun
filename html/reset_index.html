Reset Page

Reset your roll number or email ID to re-associate it!

  1. Enter your roll number below
  2. You will receive an email on the associated email adddress
  3. Click on the link in this email to remove the association between your roll number and that email address
  4. After this, you can visit the Home page to re-associate your roll number with another email address.

  1. Enter your email address below
  2. You will receive an email on this address
  3. Click on the link in this email to remove the association between this email and any roll number
  4. After this, you can visit the Home page to re-associate your email ID with another roll number
